## Body copy
Poor indoor air quality affects the health of each of us. For children, it can affect their neurodevelopment and cognitive ability negatively.

Indoor air quality problems in schools have been linked to drowsiness, headaches, loss of concentration, and asthma. It also increases absenteeism and leads to children falling behind academically. Additionally, class sizes that are predetermined by state boards of education make it more difficult to distance one child from another.

Whether you need clean air in K-12 schools, elementary or primary schools, secondary schools, colleges, universities, trade schools or an entire school district, ActivePure® has a solution to meet your facilities' unique needs.

The system does not just purify the air, it sanitizes all surfaces. HVAC-installed units as well as portables allow each school to customize a system to meet its specific needs. In older school buildings with poor ventilation, portable units can get to work immediately to sanitize exposed surfaces and the air in occupied spaces without the cost of overhauling an HVAC system. A school can simply place an ActivePure® unit in each classroom, plugging it into a regular wall outlet. It is that easy.

The system includes marketing materials to promote your investment in your school and educate your team members, educators, students, and parents about the benefits for all.

Grants & Funding are available for educational facilities. Navigate to our Funding Page (/funding) and contact us anytime for more information. We are happy to help you decide which air purification system is best for your school facility.

GERM SOLUTIONS USA believes that school administrators, teachers, staff members, and children have the right to get an education in a clean-air learning environment.
