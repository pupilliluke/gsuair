## CASE STUDY (gsuair.com/cambria-county-gov) — hidden page, linked from Government Facilities
**Headline:** LEHIGH, YORK, & CAMBRIA COUNTIES' CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES
Subhead: PA PRISON FACILITIES FIND SOLUTION FOR THEIR POOR INDOOR AIR QUALITY

Germ Solutions USA has taken significant steps to enhance safety measures within the prison facilities of Lehigh, York, and Cambria Counties in Pennsylvania.

**Installation of Air & Surface Purification Systems** — Germ Solutions USA installed their InductPro Series devices within the ductwork of the facilities. These devices release molecules that actively target and disable COVID-19 and other pathogens in the air and on surfaces, integrating into the existing infrastructure to create a safer environment for staff, inmates and visitors.

**Implementation of Indoor Air Quality Monitoring System 360** — Enables prison administrators to continuously monitor air quality in real-time, allowing early detection of issues and prompt action.

Each facility will undergo 12 months of pathogen testing to demonstrate effectiveness, providing data on the reduction of pathogens including COVID-19, and showcasing the long-term benefits and efficacy of the solutions.
