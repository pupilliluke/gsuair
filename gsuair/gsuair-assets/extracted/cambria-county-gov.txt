[h1] LEHIGH, YORK, & CAMBRIA COUNTIES' CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES
[h4] PA PRISON FACILITIES FIND SOLUTION FOR THEIR POOR INDOOR AIR QUALITY
Germ Solution Service USA has taken significant steps to enhance safety measures within the prison facilities of Lehigh, York , and Cambria Counties in Pennsylvania. Here's a detailed breakdown based on the provided information:
Installation of Air & Surface Purification Systems
Germ Solution Service USA has installed their InductPro Series devices within the ductwork of the mentioned prison facilities. These devices are designed to release molecules that actively target and disable COVID-19 and other pathogens present in the air and on surfaces. By integrating these systems into the existing infrastructure, the company aims to create a safer environment for staff, inmates and visitors.
Implementation of Indoor Air Quality Monitoring System 360
In addition to the purification systems, Germ Solution Service USA has installed their Indoor Air Quality Monitoring System 360. This technology enables prison administrators to continuously monitor the air quality within the facilities in real-time. This proactive approach allows for early detection of any potential issues and ensures prompt action can be taken to maintain a healthy indoor environment.
To demonstrate the effectiveness of the installed systems, each facility will undergo 12 months of pathogen testing. This comprehensive testing process will provide valuable data on the reduction of pathogens, including COVID-19, within the prison environment. By conducting this testing over an extended period, Germ Solution Service USA aims to showcase the long-term benefits and efficacy of their solutions.
Overall, Germ Solution Service USA's initiatives demonstrate a commitment to leveraging innovative technologies to address the challenges posed by COVID-19 and other pathogens within prison facilities. By combining purification systems with real-time monitoring and thorough testing protocols, they are contributing to the creation of safer and healthier environments for all stakeholders involved
